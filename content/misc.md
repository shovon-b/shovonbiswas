I represented Bangladesh in the [International Physics Olympiads (IPhO)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Physics_Olympiad) in Estonia (2012) and Denmark (2013).

Later, I was involved in the training and team selection of the Bangladesh National Team participating in IPhO for 2014-2016.

Since 2022, I am involved in the writing of [The Canadian Association of Physics exam](https://cap.ca/programs/recognitions/high-school-students/cegep-prize-exam/cap-hs-prize-exam/) and the selection of Canadian team to participate in IPhO.

Some problems I wrote for those exams can be found at

- [CAP](https://outreach.phas.ubc.ca/exams-and-competitions/cap-high-school-prize-exam/cap-english/)

- [BdPhO](https://www.bdpho.org/past-paper)
